Instructions of the game:

On the next page, a number of colored squares will appear on the screen.
You must observe them and remember their location. After 5 seconds the squares will flip and turn blue and start moving on the screen.
You must match each pair of squares behind which the same colors are hidden. The clock at the bottom of the page will count your time, try to break the personal record that will appear there.

On the current page, enter your username. If you are a new user, select the "Yes" option. If not select "No".
In the level field, select the game level you want:
Easy: 4 squares will appear on the screen. Medium 6 squares will appear on the screen. Hard: 8 squares will appear on the screen.
Note - the game requires a strong memory and reaction speed.

Good luck!!
